## 🏗️ The 4 Pillars of Architecture

### 1. 🌐 The Agency Frontend (Client-Facing Mastery)
* **Smart Package Builder:** A highly interactive, multi-step engine where clients can visually build their project scope (Core Foundation, Frontend Modules, AI services) and get dynamic, real-time price estimations.
* **Dynamic Portfolio & CMS:** A heavily customized CMS powering the portfolio, services, team directory, and Lens Station (studio bookings) with stunning, fluid animations and ultra-responsive UI/UX.
* **Subscription & SaaS Billing:** Supports mixed revenue models, including flat monthly retainers (AI Starter/Growth) and Pay-As-You-Go services.

### 2. 🛡️ Customer Portal (The Client Workspace)
* **Real-Time Project Telemetry:** Clients can track the live status of ongoing projects, previous deliveries, and active milestones.
* **Integrated Financial Hub:** Automated invoice generation with direct **SSLCommerz** payment gateway integration. Clients can pay directly from their dashboard and access full payment history and downloadable PDFs.
* **Support & Consultation:** Native ticketing system for project support and an integrated module to schedule Google Meet/WhatsApp consultations directly with the assigned team.

### 3. 👥 HRMS & Employee Dashboard (Operational Core)
* **Digital Onboarding & Compliance:** Secure document upload (CV, NID, Academic Certificates) during onboarding. Super Admins assign roles, offer letters, working hours, and custom breaks.
* **Time & Productivity Tracking:** Native Check-In, Check-Out, and Break monitoring. Employees *must* submit a daily activity log during check-out.
* **Automated Payroll Engine:** The system calculates salaries entirely automatically based on check-in logs, approved paid/unpaid leaves, late penalties, and base salary logic. One-click salary disbursement.
* **Task Routing:** Employees view assigned tasks, support tickets, and project briefs. They can update statuses, upload deliverables, or transfer tickets if expert intervention is needed.

### 4. 👑 Super Admin ERP (The Brain)
* **Granular RBAC:** Complete control over Super Admins, Admins, Employees, and Customers with strict data isolation.
* **Financial Ledger & Invoicing:** Complete overview of company finances, automated invoice PDF generation, and revenue tracking.
* **Custom Email Engine:** A dynamic HTML email template builder featuring variable injection (e.g., `{{employee_name}}`, `{{break_duration}}`) to automate every single operational notification.
* **Lead & Sales Pipeline:** Full CRM capabilities to track cold calls, booked meetings, and lead conversions through customized Kanban boards.

## 📈 Security, Tracking & Scalability
* **Event Deduplication:** Enterprise-grade tracking utilizing both **Meta Pixel (Browser)** and **Meta CAPI (Server-Side via Supabase)** with strict deduplication logic.
* **Cloud Asset Management:** Deep integration with **Cloudinary** for heavily optimized, fast-loading visual assets across the platform.
* **Dynamic SEO Architecture:** Auto-generating sitemaps, dynamic `robots.txt`, and meta-tag management for all CMS-driven pages.
